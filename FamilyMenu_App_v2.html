🔗 Google Sheets Connection

Paste your Cloudflare Worker URL (or Apps Script URL) below. This connects the app to your family menu Google Sheet.

📋 Setup Instructions

  1. Upload FamilyMenu_GoogleSheet.xlsx to Google Drive. Right-click → Open with → Google Sheets.
  2. In Google Sheets, click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete existing code, paste in FamilyMenu_AppsScript.gs, set your Spreadsheet ID, and click Save.
  3. Click Deploy → New deployment. Type: Web app · Execute as: Me · Who has access: Anyone. Click Deploy and authorise.
  4. (Optional but recommended) Set up a Cloudflare Worker as a CORS proxy pointing to your Apps Script URL. Paste the Worker URL here instead.
  5. Paste the URL above and tap Connect.
